Anne Frank

About Anne Frank

Anne Frank's diary is one of the most famous accounts of the Holocaust. Written while hiding from the Nazis during World War II, Anne's diary reveals her innermost thoughts, fears, and hopes during a time of extreme hardship. This website aims to provide a glimpse into Anne Frank's world, as well as her legacy.

Early Life

Anne Frank was born in 1929 in Frankfurt, Germany. In 1933, her family moved to Amsterdam to escape the growing Nazi regime. After the German invasion of the Netherlands, Anne and her family went into hiding in the secret annex of her father’s office building, where she would spend two years writing in her diary.

The Diary of Anne Frank

Anne Frank's diary was published posthumously in 1947. The book has since been translated into over 70 languages and is one of the most widely read books in the world. Anne's honest and poignant account of her life in hiding has inspired millions.
